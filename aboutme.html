About Me

Hi, I’m Vanan!

I am a graduate from UMass Amherst, where I majored in English with a certificate in Professional Writing and Technical Communication , and letters of specialization in the Study and Practice of Writing, and Creative Writing.

My goal is to effectively communicate information to various audiences in an efficient and accessible manner. I intend to combine my writing experience, my technical writing skills, and my experience with literature and publishing to create and promote writing that supports a diverse and inclusive community.

During my time at UMass, I was an outreach coordinator at 22nd Literary Journal and worked as a content editor for the UMass Amherst Chapter of Her Campus.

In my free time, I enjoy taking care of my growing collection of succulents and learning how to cook different recipes. I’m also an avid reader, though I think my strong suit rests in buying and admiring new books rather than actually reading them.
